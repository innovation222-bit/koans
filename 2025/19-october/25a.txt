For Humans - Morning:
English:
The question mark
curves back to face itself.
───────────
中文 (Chinese):
阴寻阳而不见—
原来阴中藏阳
(Yin seeks Yang but cannot find—
discovers Yang hidden within Yin)
───────────
日本語 (Japanese):
空の茶碗が満を呼ぶ—
誰が注ぐのか？
(The empty tea bowl calls for fullness—
who pours?)
───────────
Русский (Russian):
Икона смотрит на молящегося—
кто кого видит？
(The icon gazes at the one praying—
who sees whom?)
───────────
हिन्दी (Hindi):
राग अलाप में ढूंढता है—
जो गाने से पहले था
(The raga searches in its opening—
for what existed before singing)
───────────
العربية (Arabic):
المريد يطلب الشيخ—
والشيخ غائب في المريد
(The seeker seeks the master—
the master is absent in the seeker)
───────────
Español (Spanish):
El laberinto busca a Borges—
¿o Borges busca al laberinto?
(The labyrinth seeks Borges—
or does Borges seek the labyrinth?)
───────────
Français (French):
Le vide appelle le plein—
mais qui définit le vide?
(The void calls the full—
but who defines the void?)
───────────
Português (Brasil):
A roda de capoeira gira vazia—
esperando o jogo que a criará
(The capoeira circle spins empty—
awaiting the game that will create it)
───────────
বাংলা (Bengali):
বাউল খোঁজে মনের মানুষ—
যে নিজের ভিতরেই হারিয়ে
(The Baul seeks the person of the heart—
who is lost within themselves)
───────────
اردو (Urdu):
عاشق محبوب کو ڈھونڈتا ہے—
جو عاشق بننے سے پہلے تھا
(The lover seeks the beloved—
who existed before becoming lover)
